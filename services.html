Metal Roofing

Metal roofing is a popular and versatile roofing option known for its durability, longevity, and aesthetic appeal.

Rehabillitation works

Rehabilitation works refer to the process of restoring or renovating a building or structure to its original or desired condition.

Expansion joint

An expansion joint is a specialized component used in construction to accommodate the movement and expansion of structures.

Epoxy Flooring

Epoxy flooring is a type of floor coating that is known for its durability, chemical resistance, and aesthetic appeal. It consists of a combination of epoxy resin and a hardener.

Bitumen Membrane

Bitumen membranes, also known as a bituminous membrane, is a type of waterproofing material that is highly effective at preventing water penetration and protecting structures from moisture damage.

Swimmming Pool

Swimming pool waterproofing is a critical aspect of pool construction and maintenance to prevent water leakage and maintain the structural integrity of the pool. Regular inspections is required for pools.

Weather Proofing

Weatherproofing is the process of making a building resistant to the elements of weather, such as rain, wind, snow, etc.

Painting and Tiles

Painting and tiling are common finishing works done during building renovations or new construction projects

Cable Trenching

A cable trench, is designed to house and protect electrical cables and wiring inindustries, commercial spaces, etc.

Heat Reflection Coating

Heat reflection coatings are designed to reduce the amount of heat absorbed by a building. These coatings typically contain reflective pigments or particles that reflect sunlight and heat, reducing the temperature of the building.

Pressure Grouting

Pressure grouting or injection grouting, is a method used to fill voids, cracks, or gaps in concrete structures or soil. It involves injecting a grout material under pressure into the desired areas to improve the structural integrity

Water Tank

Similar to swimming pools, Water tank waterproofing is also a critical process. It helps prevents leakage, maintain water quality, and ensure the durability of the tank. This also requires regular inspections and maintainance procedures.
