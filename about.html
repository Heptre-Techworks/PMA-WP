Our History

OUR FOOTING IN THE INDUSTRY

Welcome to PMAWP, the gold standard in comprehensive waterproofing services and solutions across the globe. With a history steeped in over three decades of expert knowledge and proficiency, our establishment is an embodiment of industry-leading excellence. Our story began 16 years ago, but the wisdom we bring to our craft is built on over 33 years of cumulative experience among our specialist team.

Here at PMAWP, we're not just a company, but a consortium of seasoned professionals who have dedicated their lives to mastering the art and science of safeguarding structures against water damage. Our service range extends beyond waterproofing, and includes fireproofing, construction, and more, serving a wide array of sectors including industrial and residential.

What sets us apart is our global footprint. We are proud to have built a broad international clientele, demonstrating our ability to meet and exceed expectations, irrespective of geographical or climatic conditions. Our adaptability, combined with our time-tested techniques and innovative solutions, has catapulted us to the forefront of our industry.

Our team remains committed to providing top-tier services without compromising on quality or safety, a commitment that has made us the preferred choice for countless clients.
