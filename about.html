OUR FOOTING IN THE INDUSTRY

Welcome to PMAWP, the gold standard in comprehensive waterproofing services and solutions across the globe. With a history steeped in over three decades of expert knowledge and proficiency, our establishment is an embodiment of industry-leading excellence. Our story began 16 years ago, but the wisdom we bring to our craft is built on over 30 years of cumulative experience among our specialist team.
